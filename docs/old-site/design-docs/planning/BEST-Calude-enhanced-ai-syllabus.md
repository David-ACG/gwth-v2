### The 6 Primitives Cheat Sheet
1. **Content Creation:** Generate text, images, video, and audio

3. **Coding:** Build, debug, and optimize software
4. **Data Analysis:** Process, visualize, and interpret data
5. **Ideation & Strategy:** Brainstorm, plan, and strategize

### Key Success Factors
- Start simple, avoid complexity
- Leadership support is crucial
- Focus on high-impact, low-effort wins
- Measure and communicate value
- Build incrementally
- Invest in change management

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Over-engineering solutions
- Ignoring change management
- Lack of clear metrics
- Insufficient training
- Poor data governance
- Unrealistic expectations

### ROI Calculation Framework
- Time savings × hourly rate
- Error reduction × cost per error
- Revenue improvements
- Cost reductions
- Quality improvements
- Customer satisfaction gains
