# Not Ready to Subscribe? Start Here.

You're curious about AI but not sure if a course is the right move yet. That's completely fine.

We built two things specifically for people in your position: free labs you can try right now, and a weekly newsletter that keeps you current on what actually matters in AI. No account required for the newsletter. No credit card required for the labs. No pressure either way.

## Free Labs

The free labs are real, hands-on projects -- the same quality as the ones inside the full course. We didn't water them down or leave out the good parts. You pick a lab, follow the plain-English instructions, and walk away with something you built yourself.

No coding required. Everything is done through AI tools using natural language.

**What you might build:**

- Your first working app, built entirely with AI
- A data dashboard that pulls together information you actually care about
- An automated workflow that handles a repetitive task for you
- A research assistant that saves you hours of manual searching

You keep everything you create. The labs are yours -- finished projects, templates, workflows, all of it.

Each lab takes between 30 minutes and two hours. You can do them in any order, skip the ones that don't interest you, and come back whenever you want.

## The Newsletter

One email per week. Here's what's in it:

**A practical AI tip you can use that day.** Not theory, not hype -- a specific thing you can try in the next ten minutes that makes your work easier or faster.

**Tool updates from the GWTH Tech Radar.** We track 47+ AI tools daily so you don't have to. When something changes -- a new feature, a price increase, a better alternative -- you'll hear about it before most people notice.

**Course updates and new content previews.** If we release a new lab or update a lesson based on a tool change, subscribers hear about it first.

**What we don't do:**

- No spam. One email, once a week.
- No sales pressure. We'll mention the course exists. We won't badger you about it.
- No vendor partnerships. Every recommendation is independent. If we say a tool is good, it's because we tested it and it's good.

Unsubscribe any time, one click, no guilt trip.
