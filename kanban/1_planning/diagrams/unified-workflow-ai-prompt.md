```

```

### Style Parameters

- Resolution: 1024x1536 (portrait orientation for vertical flow)
- Style: flat-lay knolling, educational infographic
- Background: clean, light gray textured
- Arrows: hand-drawn black
- Perspective: overhead, bird's-eye view
- Lighting: soft, even, minimal shadows

### Notes

This prompt is designed for AI image generation models. The output will be a raster image (PNG/JPEG) — not an editable diagram. The flat-lay/knolling style uses physical objects arranged overhead to explain abstract concepts, similar to the water cycle example from the Linear issue.

For best results with text labels, keep them short (under 15 characters) or consider generating without text and overlaying labels programmatically afterward.
